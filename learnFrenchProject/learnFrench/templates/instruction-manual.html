Instruction manual


  • In order to use this app you must be a registered user.
  • Afterwards, you can find all of the units on the home page.
  • Inside of the units there are lessons. Every lesson has a test after it, so you can test to see if you've learned the material.
  • Inside of the test, you can click the "Check" button to check your answers, and the "Finish test" button will take you back to the home page.
  • In the upper right corner you can click the "Profile" button.
  • Once you open your profile, you'll find a set daily goal dropdown, to define how many lessons you want to learn today.
  • If you don't complete your daily goal you'll have a message on your profile that tells you that. You will also have a message once you finish it.
